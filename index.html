1. Introduction

Welcome to FedEx Delivery Service, your trusted partner for international and domestic deliveries. Whether you're sending personal items, commercial goods, or time-sensitive packages, we are here to make the process seamless and secure.

2. Categories of Shipments

Shipments are grouped to determine customs duty, processing time, and inspection needs. Use this guide to correctly classify your item:

  • Personal Items: Gifts, clothing, personal electronics. Declare as "Personal Use".
  • Business/Commercial: Products for resale, bulk items. Declare as "Commercial".
  • Electronics: Phones, laptops, appliances. Declare as "Electronic Equipment".
  • Food & Pharmaceuticals: Packaged food, supplements, medications. Declare as "Consumables".
  • Auto Parts/Vehicles: Engines, tyres, spare parts. Declare as "Automobile Equipment".

‚úÖ Tip: Declare truthfully. Misclassification may cause delays or penalties.

3. Tax & Customs Duty Guidelines (Ghana Entry)

Duties and VAT are applied based on declared value and category. These are standard government-imposed fees (not by FedEx Delivery Service).

  • Clothing & Personal Use: 10-20% customs duty. VAT applies.
  • Laptops & Phones: 5-20% customs duty. VAT applies.
  • Vehicles (used): 5-20% + age-based tax. VAT applies.
  • Pharmaceuticals: Often exempt from duties; VAT may apply depending on FDA clearance.
  • Gifts under $50: May be exempt from duties and VAT in some cases.
